The simple way to manage
product feedback

SignalBox is a unified inbox for user feedback that identifies patterns and highlights exactly what you should build next. You stop guessing and start building.

1. Collect

We bring every piece of feedback into one place without you needing to change your workflow.

  • Email Forwarding: Auto-forward support emails directly to us.
  • In-App Widget: Capture requests directly from your users.
  • Integrations: Connect Discord/Slack to capture community chat.

2. Organize

SignalBox cleans the data so you don't have to manage a messy spreadsheet.

  • Smart Grouping: We detect when users ask for the same thing differently.
  • Auto-Tagging: Feedback is tagged by topic and sentiment automatically.
  • Duplicate Merging: We merge repetitive requests to keep your view clean.

3. Decide

We turn raw data into a decision-making tool.

  • Volume Tracking: See exactly how many people requested a feature.
  • Trend Detection: Identify momentum vs. stale requests.
  • Prioritization: Push the winners to your issue tracker (Linear/GitHub).

What SignalBox is NOT

Not a Help Desk

We don't replace Intercom. Use that to talk to users; use us to track what they said.

Not JIRA

We are not for project management. Once you decide to build it, push it to your engineering tool.

Not a Chatbot

We do not reply to your users automatically. We only listen and organize.

Core Features

Universal Capture

Accepts input from email, chat apps, widgets, and API.

Semantic Grouping

Recognizes that different phrasings often mean the same thing.

Sentiment Analysis

Flags urgent frustration or negative trends immediately.

User Profiles

Trend Reports

Visualizes if a request is trending up or down over time.

Roadmap Sync

One-click export to Linear, GitHub Issues, or Trello.

SignalBox IS for you if...

  • You are a solo founder or small team (<10).
  • You handle your own support.
  • You currently use a spreadsheet/Notion.

SignalBox is NOT for you if...

  • You have a support team of 5+ agents.
  • You need complex enterprise SLAs.
  • You want a public roadmap portal.
