Our Privacy Promise

We sell software, not data. We do not sell your information to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties. We treat your data with the same respect we expect for our own.

At SignalBox, we help you make sense of your user feedback. To do that, we need to process some data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and—most importantly—what we don't do with it.

1. What data we collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and billing details required to maintain your account.
  • Feedback Data: The raw content (emails, chat logs, tickets) you explicitly connect to SignalBox from your integrations (like Discord, Slack, or Gmail).

2. How we use your data

We use your data strictly to provide the SignalBox service. This includes:

  • Analyzing your Feedback Data to group similar requests and detect trends.
  • Generating summaries and insights for your internal dashboard.
  • Sending you notifications about critical issues or spikes in feedback.

3. What we do NOT do

This section is important. To be absolutely clear:

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your private Feedback Data to train public AI models (like ChatGPT, Claude, or open-source models). Your data stays isolated to your workspace context.
  • We do not show your data to other customers.

4. Data storage & security

Security is not an afterthought. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Our infrastructure is hosted on secure cloud providers with strict access controls. Only a tiny subset of engineering staff has access to the production database for debugging purposes, and access is logged.

5. Data retention

You control your data retention. By default, we store Feedback Data to maintain your historical trends. You can delete your workspace at any time, which permanently wipes your data from our active databases immediately and from our backups within 30 days.

6. Third-party services

We use a select few trusted sub-processors to run our infrastructure (e.g., for hosting, payment processing, and LLM inference). We ensure all vendors act as data processors with strict confidentiality agreements and zero-retention policies for inference where applicable.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. You can perform most of these actions directly within your Workspace Settings. If you need help, just email us.
