Product Privacy Terms

Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Period.

We built SignalBox to help you make sense of your feedback, not to harvest your data. This policy is written in plain English because transparency is part of our product philosophy.

What data we collect

We only collect what is necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you sign up.
  • Feedback Data: The raw content you import (emails, chat logs, linear tickets, etc.) so we can analyze it.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics on how you interact with our UI to help us improve the experience.

How we use your data

Your data is used strictly to power the features of SignalBox:

  • To group similar feedback items into "Insights".
  • To generate confidence scores and trend analysis.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or critical updates.

AI & Data Training Important

We use Large Language Models (LLMs) to process your feedback and generate insights. However:

We do NOT use your private customer data to train public AI models.

Your data is processed in an isolated context. We utilize enterprise-grade APIs with zero-retention policies, meaning they do not store or learn from your data.

Data Storage & Security

We take security seriously. Your data is encrypted at rest (in our database) and in transit (via HTTPS). We restrict internal access to production data strictly to engineering needs for debugging.

Retention & Deletion

If you delete your SignalBox account, your data is removed from our live databases immediately. Backups are cycled out over a standard retention period (usually 30 days) and then permanently deleted.

Our Privacy Promise

SignalBox is a paid product, not an advertising platform. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or your customers' feedback data to third parties. Ever.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time directly through the application settings. If you need manual assistance, just email us.
